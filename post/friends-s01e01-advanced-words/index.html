I ended up living with this albino guy who was cleaning windshields…

The word “albino” refers to a person or animal with a condition called albinism, which is characterized by a lack of pigment in the skin, hair, and eyes. This results in very light skin, white or pale hair, and eyes that can appear pink or blue due to the visibility of blood vessels. In the context of the sentence from “Friends,” it describes a person with these physical traits¹.

Here’s a simple explanation in English:

  • Albino: A person with very pale skin, white hair, and light-colored eyes due to a lack of pigment.

And here’s how you might explain it in Chinese (since your system settings indicate a preference for Chinese):

This condition is not just a human trait; animals can be albinos too. The term can also be used as an adjective to describe organisms with this condition¹.
